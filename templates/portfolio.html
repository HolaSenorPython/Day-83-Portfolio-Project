Day 90: Disappearing Text App

This app was super fun to make and an interesting concept. Basically for this day, and similar for every day in the course from Day 83 upwards, there were no videos or instructions, merely an instruction for what to make and kinda what was expected. This app essentially is a Python powered Tkinter-based app that allows the user to type whatever they want, however they want, for about 3 seconds before erasing the text. The idea behind it is that it destroys writer's block, because it forces the writer to continue writing. I thought it was a fascinating concept, and it wasn't insanely hard to build.
